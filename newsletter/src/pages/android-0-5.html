Cryptomator for Android 0.5 Beta

OneDrive and WebDAV support is here! And we've added German translation. Release notes are available at cryptomator.org/android.

Just a heads-up: WebDAV is still missing support for self-signed certificates. This will be added with an upcoming patch. In addition, not all WebDAV servers, especially ones which issue redirects, are supported.

Thank you for all your feedback so far! Keep them coming. :) We're open for feature requests, suggestions, and obviously bug reports.

You can send us feedback on GitHub. Please review and follow our contribution guidelines.

Disclaimer: Cryptomator for Android is beta software. By downloading Cryptomator, you agree to only use it for testing only with recoverable data. Cryptomator contributors will not be liable for any loss or damage to your data.

Requirements: Android 4.1 (Jelly Bean) or higher
