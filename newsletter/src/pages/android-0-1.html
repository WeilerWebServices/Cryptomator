Cryptomator for Android 0.1 Beta

For this first beta version, we're only supporting Dropbox. We'll add Google Drive, OneDrive, and WebDAV in future beta versions. This version is by far not feature-complete and has known issues, but we're of course open for feature requests, suggestions, and obviously bug reports.

You can send us feedback on GitHub. We've created a repository just for collecting Android issues. Please review and follow our contribution guidelines.

Thank you all for your patience! We know some of you have been already subscribed to this newsletter for many months. We're looking forward to your feedback! :)

Disclaimer: Cryptomator for Android is beta software. By downloading Cryptomator, you agree to only use it for testing only with recoverable data. Cryptomator contributors will not be liable for any loss or damage to your data.

Requirements: Android 4.1 (Jelly Bean) or higher
