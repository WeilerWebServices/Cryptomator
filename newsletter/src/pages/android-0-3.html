Cryptomator for Android 0.3 Beta

Google Drive support is here! Many bugs have been fixed as well, which should improve the overall stability. Release notes are available at cryptomator.org/android.

Our plan is to make the next version 0.4 "feature-complete", so that it's even better integrated into the Android platform. Following that with version 0.5 and so on, we will mainly focus on adding OneDrive and WebDAV.

Thank you for all your feedback so far! Keep them coming. :) The beta version is not feature-complete and has known issues, but we're of course open for feature requests, suggestions, and obviously bug reports.

You can send us feedback on GitHub. Please review and follow our contribution guidelines.

Disclaimer: Cryptomator for Android is beta software. By downloading Cryptomator, you agree to only use it for testing only with recoverable data. Cryptomator contributors will not be liable for any loss or damage to your data.

Requirements: Android 4.1 (Jelly Bean) or higher
