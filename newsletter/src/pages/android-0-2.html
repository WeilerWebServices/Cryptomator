Cryptomator for Android 0.2 Beta

Version 0.2 includes several bug fixes and design improvements. Release notes are available at cryptomator.org/android.

We haven't added further cloud storage services yet, because we wanted to fix some major/critical bugs first (couldn't fix all of them yet; some known issues remain). Our plan is to add Google Drive next for version 0.3, and after that OneDrive and WebDAV.

Thank you for all your feedback so far! Keep them coming. :) The beta is not feature-complete and has known issues, but we're of course open for feature requests, suggestions, and obviously bug reports.

You can send us feedback on GitHub. Please review and follow our contribution guidelines.

Disclaimer: Cryptomator for Android is beta software. By downloading Cryptomator, you agree to only use it for testing only with recoverable data. Cryptomator contributors will not be liable for any loss or damage to your data.

Requirements: Android 4.1 (Jelly Bean) or higher
