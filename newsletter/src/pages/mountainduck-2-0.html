Mountain Duck Meets Cryptomator

Mountain Duck 2.0 with support for Cryptomator vaults has been released. Mountain Duck lets you mount server and cloud storage as a local disk in Finder on macOS and in File Explorer on Windows.

After the successful integration of Cryptomator in Cyberduck 6.0, we're excited that Cryptomator has been integrated into Mountain Duck as well. This gives you a new way of accessing your vaults.

Mountain Duck lets you open remote files with any application and work like on a local volume. FTP, SFTP, WebDAV, Amazon S3, OpenStack Swift, Backblaze B2, Microsoft Azure & OneDrive, Google Drive, and Dropbox are the supported protocols. All vaults created with Mountain Duck or Cryptomator can be opened with the other.

You can learn more about our cooperation with Mountain Duck at our dedicated coop page.
